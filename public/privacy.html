Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 2025

This Privacy Policy describes how DarkAge SMP (“we”, “our”, or “us”) collects, uses, and protects information related to your gameplay and participation in our community.

1. Information We Collect

We collect minimal data such as your Minecraft username, Discord ID, and gameplay statistics for the purpose of managing server access and community engagement. We do not collect sensitive personal data.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your information is used solely to operate and improve our server experience, handle bans or support requests, and enable Discord-linked features. We never sell or share your information with advertisers.

3. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect player data from unauthorized access or misuse. However, online data transmission can never be guaranteed as completely secure.

4. Third-Party Services

DarkAge SMP may use integrations such as Discord bots or third-party analytics tools. These services are governed by their own privacy policies and may collect limited technical data.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

We keep data only as long as needed to operate the server or community. You may request deletion of your data by contacting us directly.

6. Children’s Privacy

Our services are intended for players aged 13 and above, in compliance with Discord’s and Mojang’s policies. We do not knowingly collect data from minors under 13.

7. Updates to This Policy

We may revise this policy as our services evolve. Updates are posted here with a new revision date.

8. Contact
